How it works

Simple process. No confusion.

1) Tell us what’s going on

Send a quick message with what you’re trying to do (and what’s not working). Screenshots help, but not required.

2) We diagnose + give a real plan

We’ll explain what we think is happening and the cleanest way to fix it. If it’s not worth paying for, we’ll say that too.

3) We fix it + hand it off clean

You’ll leave with a working setup, plus clear next steps so you’re not calling again for the same issue.

Want the fastest help?

When you reach out, include the device type, what you were trying to do, and any error message you’re seeing.
