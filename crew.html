Tavares Baker

Aspiring Network Engineer

My name is Tavares Baker, and I’m an aspiring network engineer. Born and raised in Chicago, I was introduced to technology early on—something that sparked a curiosity that still drives me today.

I compete regularly in cybersecurity-focused competitions, which have helped me build real skills like responding to cyberattacks and removing malware from compromised devices. Long before I ever dealt with hackers, I was building my own computers, embedded systems, and servers. From there, I moved into programming and continued expanding my technical range.

I’m currently focused on growing my knowledge of network security, but my security journey started with endpoint incident response. I have a deep passion for technology and helping others, which is why I love Baker Tech Solutions—it lets me level up while making a positive impact in my community.

I’m always pushing to improve, and I want to help others grow too. Outside of tech, I enjoy reading, watching TV shows, and spending time with friends. I like to have fun, but professional priorities always come first.
