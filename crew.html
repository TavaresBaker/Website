Tavares Baker

Aspiring Network Engineer

My name is Tavares Baker, and I am an aspiring network engineer with a strong interest in learning how modern technology works behind the scenes. I enjoy exploring new platforms, configuring systems, and understanding how different tools and networks operate in real environments. What motivates me most is the idea of helping build a more advanced and technologically capable world.

I was born and raised in Chicago, Illinois, and currently live in Fort Wayne, Indiana while attending Indiana Tech as a Computer Science major. Over the past two and a half years, I have gained hands-on experience in the IT field, becoming familiar with real operational processes, compliance expectations, and the types of technical challenges organizations face every day.

When I am not working or studying, I enjoy watching TV shows, reading books, and spending time with friends. I believe in continuing to grow both professionally and personally, and I am always looking for new opportunities to expand my knowledge and improve my skills.
